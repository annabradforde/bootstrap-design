Best Café Treats

My top 5 favorite orders

Pain au Chocolat

A classic french pastry, this treat goes well with any coffee. With a flaky, chocolatey center, a pain au chocolat is a perfect treat to order at any cafe.

Madeleines

Madeleines are a delicious and simple treat to pair with coffee. Its vanilla cake center makes you want to keep ordering more, and can even be enjoyed outside the cafe.

Croissants

Croissants are a classic cafe treat. Although croissants come in various flavors and recipes, the classic simple croissant is enough to satisfy. The buttery and flaky pastry is simple yet enjoyable to order anywhere.

Coffee Cake

Coffee cakes offer a blend of cinnamon, brown sugar, and vanilla to enjoy with any coffee. The layered cakes can be enjoyed year round with your favorite cafe drink.

Eclairs

The average American cafe may not have this beloved treat, but it is by far one of the favorite desserts to enjoy worldwide. The creamy center paired with the chocolate topping is perfect as an afternoon treat with your coffee.
